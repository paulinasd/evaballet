Biography

Born in a military family, she started her ballet career in 2014 at the Lithuanian International Ballet Academy https://www.baletas.com/

In 2019, she started studying professional ballet at The National M.K. Čiurlionis School of Art https://www.cmm.lt/about-the-school/

Started studying at Zurich Dance Academy in 2022 https://www.zhdk.ch/en/taz

Taking part in the selection of young ballet talents every year, Eva received invitations to the world's best ballet schools, such as the Grand Opera de Paris, Royal Ballet Academy in London, Zurich Dance Academy, Princess Grace Ballet School and Berlin State Ballet School. By participating in the master classes of these schools, she gained experience, knowledge and ballet practice with the best teachers.
